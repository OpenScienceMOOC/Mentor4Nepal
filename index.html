What we do

#M4N Initiative proves various services related to career and professional development. Through a mentor, a mentee is guided for a fulfilled life with successful career.

Career Advising

A mentor can help you figure out what you want to do with your life. We will work with you to assess your skills, interests, and values. We will also help you identify your career goals and develop a plan to achieve them.

Thesis & Research Projects

An experienced mentor will work with you to choose a topic, develop a research proposal, conduct research, analyze data, write your thesis or research paper and successfully defend your thesis.

Conference & Journal Publications

An experienced mentor will prepare you for the right conference and journal selection, prepare & format slides or paper, support submission & peer-review process and preparation for the presentation.

Personal Statement and CV Preparation

An experienced mentor will work with you to choose the right format for CV, highlight your skills and experience, write a strong personal statement and proofread your work.
